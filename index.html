About Me

I was a Master and Ph.D. student at SNU Automotive Lab in Seoul National University (since 2006), where I worked on the topics in Diesel Engine Combustion with Prof. Kyoungdoug Min and other researchers. My research interests are diesel engine combustion, optimizing combustion bowl shapes, and optimizing combustion systems to enhance combustion efficiency and to reduce emissions. I worked as a web programmer at Neowiz in Korea Rep. (2001-2004). I received my B.S. degree in Seoul National University (2006).
Now I am a Senior engineer at Doosan Infracore (since 2013).
